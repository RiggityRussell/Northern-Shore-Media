What Is Social Media Management?

SMM is one of the fastest-growing forms of communication and promotion. It includes the use of social networking sites, such as: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc. Networking sites that help us connect with family, friends, and brands like yours - where users share images, videos, opinions, reviews, and more. By adding a social media profile to your marketing plan, you provide consumers with an opportunity to access your site, pages, and business.
